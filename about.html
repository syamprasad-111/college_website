Who We Are

Established in 1990, Prasad V. Potluri Institute of Technology is a premier educational institution committed to excellence in technical education and research. Our mission is to shape the next generation of innovators and leaders through a robust curriculum, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and hands-on learning experiences. With a strong emphasis on both academic and personal growth, we prepare students to tackle real-world challenges with confidence and creativity.

With cutting-edge laboratories, well-stocked libraries, and a vibrant campus life, PVPSIT fosters a holistic learning environment that nurtures intellectual curiosity and innovation. Our highly qualified faculty members bring a wealth of knowledge and experience, providing mentorship and guidance to empower students to achieve their full potential. The institute also actively collaborates with leading industries and organizations, ensuring that students are well-prepared to meet the dynamic demands of the professional world.
